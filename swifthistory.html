History

Swift was officially released in October of 2014 after four years of development lead by Chris Lattner. Swift took language ideas from Objective-C, Rust, Haskell, Ruby, Python, C#, CLU, and many others. A beta version of the programming language was released to registered Apple developers at the conference, but the company did not promise that the final version of Swift would be source code compatible with the test version.
